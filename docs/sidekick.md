## Introduction
The Sidekick Tool Kit is an AMS accessory set designed for FDM 3D printing enthusiasts.

The kit includes:

- Precision cutters for smooth, clean model trimming.

- A 5-in-1 screwdriver with interchangeable bits conveniently stored in the handle.

- A custom-color Panda Edge.

## Key Features

- **Convenient and Practical**: 
The 5-in-1 screwdriver meets most routine 3D printer maintenance needs.

- **Clean and Precise Cutting**:
Ultra-thin cutters with sharp edges ensure smooth, flat cuts.

## Usage Instructions

- **Cutters**

    Made of chrome-molybdenum steel with a rust-resistant finish.
    Before use, remove the protective cap. Place the flat side of the blade close to the area to be trimmed, then begin cutting.

    ⚠️ After use, place the protective cap back onto the blade to protect the blade and prevent damage.

    <img src=img/SidekickToolKit/en/2.png width="600"/>

- **5-in-1 Screwdriver**

    The cap at the end of the handle can be removed to access the magnetically stored bits (H1.5, H2.0, H2.5 and H3.0).
    Remove the default Phillips bit and insert the desired bit for use.

    <img src=img/SidekickToolKit/en/1.png width="600"/>
